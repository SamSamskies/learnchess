Hippo Capture Scan

The Captures step in CCT — for your pawn chain only.

e3 · h3 · d3

One second per square. Trace the diagonal or file from each pawn outward. Done in 3 seconds — not a calculation.

What to look for on each square

SquareCommon attackers
e3 Bishop on c5, a7 (long diagonal). After pawn breaks like f4, diagonals open — re-scan.
h3 Undeveloped bishop still on c8 (fires through the fianchetto). Bishop on g7 via h6. Queen on d8–h4 file.
d3 Queen to d3 or c4. Bishop on b5, a6. After g4 or center opens, queen hops to d3.

When to run it

  • Every move — as the Captures step inside CCT
  • Extra re-scan after pawn breaks (f4, g4, b4) — diagonals shift
  • At critical speed before piece moves that open lines (Nd2, Ne2)
