Three speeds

SpeedWhenTarget
Book Your familiar Hippo moves (first ~12 moves) ≤10 sec
Normal Quiet development, obvious recaptures 15–30 sec
Critical Pawn breaks, trades, king safety, tactics 45–120 sec
Move ~15 checkpoint: ≥6:00 on your clock

CCT is not a speed

After you pick a move at any speed, add a 3-second safety scan (CCT). Thinking time and scan time are separate — never replace one with the other.

Book moves (White Hippo example)

If you recognize the position, play in book speed — don't re-invent:

g3 · Bg2 · d3 · Nd2 · e3 · h3 · a3 · b4 · Bb2 · O-O

Critical moment triggers

  • Opponent pawn push against your chain (…f5, …e5, …d5)
  • Any capture or trade offer
  • Your king crosses the third rank
  • You drop below 3:00 with pieces still on the board
