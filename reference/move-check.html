Checks · Captures · Threats

After you pick a move, pause. Ask what your opponent can do next:

  1. Checks — Can they give check? Where does my king go?
  2. Captures — Can they take something? Is what I left undefended?
  3. Threats — Can they fork, pin, or threaten mate in one?

When to use it

  • Every move in Duolingo (10 min — no time to recover)
  • Every move once you're below 2 minutes on the clock
  • Always before captures, checks, and pawn pushes

When to skip the deep think

  • Obvious recaptures — still do a 2-second CCT
  • Known opening moves in your Hippo setup
  • Forced replies when only one legal move exists
