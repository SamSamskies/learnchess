# Chess Glossary

Terms promoted here are ones you've demonstrated understanding of in lessons. More terms will be added as you learn them.

## Habits

**CCT (Checks, Captures, Threats)**:
A brief pre-move safety scan — ask what your opponent can do immediately after your intended move.
_Avoid_: Blunder check (too vague), sanity check

**Safety scan**:
A fast look for one-move dangers, distinct from deep calculation of the whole position.
_Avoid_: Full analysis, calculating everything
